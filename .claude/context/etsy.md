# PosterGenius — Etsy Store

## Store

- **URL:** https://www.etsy.com/shop/PosterGeniusCA
- **Platform role:** Discovery + buyer trust + built-in marketplace traffic

## Fee Structure (know before pricing)

| Fee | Rate |
|---|---|
| Listing fee | $0.20 USD per listing (renews every 4 months) |
| Transaction fee | 6.5% of sale price + shipping |
| Payment processing | ~3% + $0.25 (Etsy Payments) |
| Total effective take | ~10–12% per sale |

Price listings to maintain healthy margins after fees.

## Etsy SEO Priorities

- **Titles:** front-load the most searched keyword (e.g., "Minimalist Botanical Print | Printable Wall Art 12x18")
- **Tags:** use all 13 tags, mix broad + specific terms
- **Thumbnails:** clean, high-contrast mockup images — this is the #1 click driver
- **Category + attributes:** fill in every field Etsy provides (material, size, style, room, etc.)
- **Renewal cadence:** fresh listings get a brief boost — consider relisting stale items

## Listing Strategy

- Offer multiple size variants per listing (12×18, 18×24, 24×36)
- Separate listings for digital vs. physical versions (different buyer intent)
- Bundle related prints as multi-pack listings (higher AOV)
- Cross-link to postergenius.ca in shop bio (not in listing descriptions — Etsy TOS)

## Growth Levers

1. **Thumbnail quality** — single biggest driver of click-through on Etsy
2. **Tag research** — use eRank or Marmalead to find high-traffic, low-competition tags
3. **New listing velocity** — Etsy rewards shops that list regularly
4. **Star Seller badge** — requires 95%+ 5-star reviews, fast replies, on-time shipping
5. **Etsy Ads** — only activate once organic is working (start at $1–3/day per top listing)
